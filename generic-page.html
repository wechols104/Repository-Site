Primary Research

Objective: Collect research about your client as they exist today. This should focus on the website that you will be working on, but also focus on other design and marketing collateral that has been produced by your client. Include existing branded elements that they may have published online, print collateral, guidelines, advertisements, social media, and any other relevant material. This will help you understand how your client exists in the broader community, and how they portray themselves in other contexts - not just digital.

What they do: Founded in 2004, Games for Change facilitates the creation and distribution of social impact games that serve as critical tools in humanitarian and educational efforts.

Challenges: Games for Change runs game design challenges for the public and professional communities, Fellowships programs that engage college/university students, and a year-round impact game design program and competition for middle and high school students, called the G4C Student Challenge.

Consulting: G4C partners with organizations seeking support for a range of services involving games and impact. G4C offers strategic workshops, vendor selection, project management and executive production. Collaborating with designers, academics, subject matter experts and researchers alike, G4C provides vision, continuity and support through the duration of the project.
